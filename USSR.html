USSR's
IS-2 and T-34
Tanks




IS-2 Tank
1.Type: Soviet heavy tank.
2.Armament: 122 mm D-25T main gun.
3.Armor: Up to 120 mm thick.
4.Use: Effective in battles like the Battle of Berlin.
5.Strengths: Powerful gun, strong frontal armor.
6.Weaknesses: Slower, less maneuverable.
T-34 Tank
1.Type: Soviet medium tank.
2.Armament: 76.2 mm gun (later 85 mm in T-34-85).
3.Armor: Sloped design for increased protection.
4.Use: Key in battles like the Battle of Kursk.
5.Strengths: Good mobility, balanced design, reliable.
6.Weaknesses: Early models had poor optics, cramped interior.
Summary
1.IS-2: Heavy, powerful tank for breaking enemy lines.
2.T-34: Versatile, influential medium tank key to Soviet success.
